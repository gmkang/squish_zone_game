Instructions
Eeek! A horde of insects have invaded your home. Click on the bugs to swat them as fast as you can before they swarm through. Collect 150 points to win a highly potent can of bug spray. Once you see the "Buzz Off" spray on the bottom right corner, simply press the spacebar to activate this killer concoction and mouseover those annoying little critters. Kill them all before the timer runs out and they takeover your home...
